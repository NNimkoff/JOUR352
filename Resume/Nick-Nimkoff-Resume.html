About Me


Nicholas "Nick" Nimkoff is 21 year old a Journalism student at UMD, currently double majoring in Film Studies. He is from Orlando, FL and graduated from Lake Highland Prepatory School in 2018. His currently living in the College Park, MD area where the University of Maryland is located, very close to Washington D.C. He has had a passion for film since a young age and follows the film industry closely, keeping up with whatever the latest in entertainment may be. He is also a dedicated journalist, whether through podcast or blog posts Nick loves to write about film and media. He has grand ambitions to join the entertainment journalism industry and following in the footsteps of the great Rodger Ebert as his inspiration. He has spent years informing himself on film on a global scale, seeing any movie he could from every country he could to help inform him on the state of the entire industry. He has written for many of UMD's publications, including the Diamondback, writing stories about the community and the entertainment that can be found all around campus. He also enjoys writing about video games, amusement parks, music, art, television and comic book culture. He is currently in his junior year and plans to graduate Spring 2022.
